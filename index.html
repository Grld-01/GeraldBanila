Gerald Banila

Hello, I'm Gerald B. Banila, a graduate of PUP Binan and a passionate web developer. I have a strong affinity for art, which I believe greatly influences my approach to web design and development. My career journey began as a web developer, where I immersed myself in the intricacies of the development side, learning and growing with each project. Now, my career goal is to transition into the role of a Project Manager, with the aim of leading and coordinating teams and projects to ensure client satisfaction. My journey from a web developer to a prospective Project Manager has been an exciting and educational one, and I look forward to continuing to expand my skill set in the field.
